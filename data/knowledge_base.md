## Saudi Arabia E-Commerce Market
<!-- category: market_overview | source: Mordor Intelligence | market: Saudi Arabia -->

## Electronics E-Commerce Benchmarks
<!-- category: electronics_specific | source: Shopify/Statista/ECDB | market: Global & Saudi -->
- Average order value: 300-800 SAR for accessories, 800-3000 SAR for devices
- Return rate: 8-12% (higher than average due to defects/compatibility)
- Repeat purchase rate: 10-20% — customers buy accessories after main device
- Best-selling accessories: chargers, cables, cases (high margin, high volume)
- Cross-sell rate: 35-45% buy accessories with main device
- Cart abandonment: 71-74% (lower than fashion due to research-heavy buying)
- Conversion rate: 1.5-2.5% (lower due to high price consideration time)
- Mobile share of sales: 65-70% in Saudi Arabia
- Peak sales: White Friday, Back to School, New Year, Ramadan
- Average customer lifetime value: 1500-4000 SAR over 2 years
- Bundle conversion boost: 20-30% higher when accessories bundled with device

## Customer Lifetime Value & Segmentation

- Loyalty program members spend 12-18% more than non-members

## Best Practices — E-Commerce Saudi Arabia
<!-- category: best_practices | source: Shopify/Smart Insights | market: Saudi Arabia -->
- Free shipping threshold increases average order value by 20-30%
- Bundle offers increase average order value by 15-25%
- Limited time offers boost conversion by 25-35%
- Product reviews increase purchase likelihood by 30%
- Mobile-optimized checkout reduces abandonment by 20%
- Arabic language support increases conversion by 15-25% in Saudi market
- Cash on delivery still preferred by 20% of Saudi shoppers (declining)
- Same-day delivery increases conversion by 25% in major Saudi cities
